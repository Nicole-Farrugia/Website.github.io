More About Me

Throughout my life, my artistic journey has been a persistent practice. Starting with classical dance, I developed a love for ballet and art at a very young age. That led me later in my life to study 'Art' and 'History of Art' at an Advanced Level. I felt the need to extend my skills beyond traditional art after finishing my Sixth Form studies, which is why I took a course in Digital Arts (Faculty of Media and knowledge Sciences) at the University of Malta. As a result, I have been more focused on graphic design , photography and illustration over the past three years.
